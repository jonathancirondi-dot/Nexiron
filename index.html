Architecting a Unified Trade Platform Connecting Manufacturers, Distributors, Wholesalers and Retailers

The Challenge

Supply chain fragmentation was costing businesses across the value chain. Manufacturers couldn't see real-time demand signals. Distributors managed orders through WhatsApp threads and spreadsheets. Retailers lacked access to competitive wholesale pricing and reliable fulfilment visibility. There was no digital infrastructure connecting these parties — regardless of whether they were SMEs, mid-market operators, or enterprise-scale businesses.

Our Approach

We scoped, architected, and built a multi-sided commerce and operations platform — purpose-built to serve all four supply chain tiers simultaneously. The platform enables live inventory visibility, automated order workflows, cross-tier pricing agreements, and integrated payment settlement. Role-based access controls allow SMEs, mid-market businesses, and enterprise operations to participate within the same ecosystem while maintaining full commercial confidentiality between parties.
